Metrics Legend & Definitions
  • Total Attempts: All attempts completed so far.
  • Successes: Attempts judged as successful.
  • Failures: Non-timeout attempts judged unsuccessful.
  • Skipped: Attempts skipped when a single step exceeds the per-step limit.
  • Timeouts: Attempts that failed due to timeout conditions.
  • Success Rate: successes / total attempts.
  • Average: Mean attempt duration.
  • Median: Middle attempt duration (50th percentile).
  • P95: 95th percentile attempt duration.
  • Duration Units: Under 120s shows seconds; 120s+ shows m s; 3600s+ shows h m s.
